Taejune Park is an Associate Professor in the School of Artificial Intelligence at Chonnam National University. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) under the supervision of Prof. Seungwon Shin. He also earned an M.S. from KAIST and a B.S. from Korea Maritime and Ocean University (KMOU). Before joining JNU in 2021, he worked as a post-doctoral researcher at KAIST and participated in the Samsung Software Membership internship program at Samsung Electronics. His research interests include security in SDN/NFV environments, IoT, and data plane architecture.
