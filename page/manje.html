Manje Kim received the Ph.D. degree from AI Graduate School, Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST), Republic of Korea, in 2023, and the M.S. degree from the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology, in 2019, and the B.S. degree in computer science from Sejong University, South Korea, in 2017, He is currently an Assistant Professor with the Convergence of AI, Chonnam National University. His research interests include Reinforcement Learning, Human-like AI, Video game AI, the algorithms for game AI and Control intelligence. He was a co-organizer of the IEEE CIG 2015 Starcraft AI Competition. He received the third place in the CIG Fighting Game AI Competition in 2015, 2017 and 2018, respectively.
