Welcome to the SRC Lab.

The Secure and Reliable Computing (SRC) Lab at Chonnam National University specializes in information security. Our current research focuses on key challenges such as cloud and network security, high-performance security systems, security for and with AI (including adversarial AI), and traffic and system analysis. We are passionate about developing innovative security architectures and systems! 😊
