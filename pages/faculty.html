Taejune Park is an Associate Professor at the College of AI Convergence, School of Artificial Intelligence at Chonnam National University (JNU). He received the Ph.D. degree from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), advised by Prof. Seungwon Shin, the M.S degree from KAIST, and the B.S. from Korea Maritime and Ocean University (KMOU), all in Computer Science/Engineering. Prior to joining JNU in 2021, he has been a post-doctoral researcher fellow at KAIST, software developer at an internship program at Samsung Electronics Co., LTD., called Samsung Software Membership. His research interests include security issues on an SDN/NFV environment and IoT (Inter of Things), and a data plane architecture.
