Welcome to the SRC Source Lab.

Led by Taejune Park and Maeje Kim the SRC (Secure and Reliable Computing) laboratory at Chonnam National University focuses on information security. In particular, we are currently focusing on research challenges including, (1) Cloud and Network security, (2) Security for/with AI (as well as adversarial AI), and (3) Traffic and system analysis. Also, we are going to study on innovative AI-based security systems as well :)
